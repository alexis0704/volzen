# Vietnam EV Charging Legal Notes

Last reviewed: 2026-06-28
Jurisdiction: Vietnam
Use: Informational only. Not legal advice.

## Knowledge Items

- id: law-electricity-retail-boundary
  sourceId: LAW-01
  claimType: external/legal
  article: Article 44
  label: Not legal advice
  date: 2026-06-28
  note: Retail electricity pricing is regulated under Vietnam's Electricity Law; Volzen must not present itself as the seller of electricity unless a qualified operator and legal review confirm the model.

- id: law-commercial-ev-charging-metering
  sourceId: LAW-02
  claimType: external/legal
  article: Article 10
  label: Not legal advice
  date: 2026-06-28
  note: Commercial EV charging electricity pricing applies to charging stations, charging pillars, and battery-swap cabinets serving paid EV charging, and separate electricity metering is required for that use.

- id: law-operator-compliance-review
  sourceId: LAW-03
  claimType: external/legal
  article: General operator compliance review
  label: Not legal advice
  date: 2026-06-28
  note: Charging-site operators should verify property permission, electrical safety, fire safety, metering, local business requirements, and pricing responsibility before opening a paid site.
